Our Story

A Little Corner of Comfort

Coffee Crave is a cozy neighborhood cafe in Bansdroni, Kolkata, known for its delicious Indo-Chinese dishes, comforting soups, flavorful noodles, burgers, and refreshing drinks. A perfect place to hang out with friends, enjoy great food, and relax.
